FORIOUS Stainless Steel Towel Bar

The FORIOUS towel bar is made from high-quality SUS304 stainless steel, ensuring long-lasting strength and resilience in any bathroom, including those with high humidity. Its brushed nickel finish not only enhances the modern aesthetic but also resists corrosion and rust, making it a durable choice for everyday use. Designed with a 12-inch center-to-center spacing, it securely holds one large bath towel or several hand towels, while its robust construction can support static loads up to 22 pounds. Whether you're upgrading your home bathroom or looking for a reliable solution for your RV, this bar offers both functionality and style.

Whether used for daily routines or as a long-term investment, this towel rack provides a sleek, contemporary look that complements modern bathroom designs and stands the test of time.
